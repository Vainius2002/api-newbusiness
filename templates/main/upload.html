Expected CSV Format:
  • Supported encodings: UTF-8, UTF-16, Latin-1, CP1252
  • Supported separators: Tab, Comma, Semicolon

  • Empty cells will be treated as 0
Important: Data Overwrite Behavior

All existing spending data for advertisers in the CSV will be completely overwritten. This means if an advertiser previously had 5 years of data but your CSV only contains 2 years, the 3 missing years will be deleted. Other advertisers not in the CSV will remain unchanged.
